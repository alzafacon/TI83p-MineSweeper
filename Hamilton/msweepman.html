ABOUT MineSweeper

 

 

MineSweeper is the classic game, brought to your calculator. Enjoy this exciting, (dare I say it again) explosive game. Have fun, and watch out for those mines!

 

 

INSTRUCTIONS

 

Key
Action
2nd
Selects/Skips Correcting
Alpha Place Flag
Arrow Keys
Move Cursor

 

The object of the game is to reveal all of the tiles that aren't mines. If you find a mine, you lose. A number means that that many adjacent spaces have a mine.
